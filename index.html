SEO and SMO Techniques

SEO Techniques

Meta Descriptions: Meta descriptions provide a brief summary of a webpage's content in search engine results. They improve click-through rates by giving users a clear idea of the page's relevance to their query (Smith, 2023).

Alt Text for Images: Alt text enhances accessibility and helps search engines understand the content of images, improving image search rankings (Doe, 2023).

Keyword Optimization: Strategically placing keywords in titles, headings, and content improves a website's relevance for specific searches (Johnson, 2023).

SMO Techniques

Facebook Like Button: Adding a Facebook Like button allows users to quickly share content with their networks, increasing visibility and engagement (Brown, 2023).

Instagram Sharing: Integrating Instagram sharing features helps showcase visual content, fostering audience interaction and brand presence (Taylor, 2023).

Twitter Hashtags: Using relevant hashtags on Twitter expands content reach by categorizing posts for broader discovery (Davis, 2023).
